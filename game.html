Welcome to Play My Game

This game is about the journey a Tibetan girl named Methok, who escapes from Tibet, walking through the Himalayas, risking both the dangers of getting caught by the Chinese police, or even getting shot by them, and getting stuck in the wilderness of the treacherous Himalayan terrains. So that she can find freedom in exile in India.

This is an adventerous scoring game where the arrow (-->, <--, ^) buttons will be used to move the methok.
